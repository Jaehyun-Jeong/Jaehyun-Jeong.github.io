In this post, I want to deepen the understanding related to RECAP(RL with Experience and Corrections via Advantage-conditioned Policies) which adopted the idea of advantage with imitation learning like actor-critic method in RL. In RECAP algorithm, advantage of actions are calculated through value network and feed this information into VLM backbone as improvement indicator. I believe that's the overall concept of this method. However, this simple idea adjusts problem of (RL + flow matching) method.

To begin with, I need to convince you to know why we need RL in the pi 0.6 model and why why combining RL with pi 0.6 was challenging. On top of that, I want to talk about the details of this method through equations.

## Why RL?

In the field of Physical Intelligence, pretrained models have shown performance improvements in a number of tasks such as folding laundry and assemble a box. Even so, pretraining + fine tune strategy was highly sensitive to the environment setting while having upperbound of data performance. In addition to that, if the robot encounter an unseen observations, it is prone to collapse due to the lack of data. However, applying RL can overcome these problems with human-intervention and self-experience. I will explain why in the later section.

## Difficulties in RL + flow matching approach

The problem: Flow matching VLAs can't use standard RL (PPO, SAC) because they don't provide action log-probabilities.

The solution: Label actions as 'positive' or 'negative' based on outcomes, train the model to generate both, then always condition on 'positive' at deployment.

The why they have applied like this.

This is basically a imitation learning with advantage from value function. And even value function is not trained with bellman equation.

Convert RL into conditional supervised learning.

RECAP enables VLA models to improve beyond demonstration quality by learning to distinguish good actions from bad ones, then always requesting 'good' at inference time.
